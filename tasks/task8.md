# Task 8: Yandex DataLens

In this assignment, you will explore data using Yandex DataLens and reflect on your analysis.

Before completing the task, build a dashboard based on a dataset (from previous tasks or your own). This step is exploratory and is not graded directly.

The main objective is to analyze the results and provide structured answers.

## Task

Write short answers (3–6 sentences per question):

### 1. Use case  
What type of problem or task is Yandex DataLens most suitable for? In what situations would you use it?

### 2. Experience  
What did you find convenient or useful when working with DataLens? What was difficult?

### 3. Visualization  
Which types of charts or dashboards did you find most effective for understanding the data? Why?

### 4. Application  
How could you use DataLens in your own work, studies, or projects? Give a specific example.

### 5. Improvements  
What would you improve in DataLens or in your workflow when using it?
